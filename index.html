Bio

Karina Sevoyan was born in 1985 in Leningrad. She spent her youth at art workshops in Tsarskoye Selo, studying painting under Alexander Nekrasov. After graduating from Saint Petersburg State University of Economics, she worked in the field of logistics. After 8 years of work in the specialty, she left financial director office because freedom and creative searching captured the Karina's mind.

For a long time she focused only on object painting, performing easel and monumental oil paintings. During the pandemic, the artist began to rethink the meaning of the forms assigned to concepts and gradually shifted her sight towards abstract painting. Adrian Genie, whom she met at his personal exhibition in the State Hermitage in 2019, strong influenced the Karina's decision to move away from objectivity.

After February 2022, she moved to Yerevan, her father's homeland, in search of her national identity. Currently, she divides her time between St.Petersburg, Yerevan and Paris.

Member of the self-organization F5 and the founder of the art group "Complices"

Artist Statement

Within the my artistic inquiry resides a dynamic interplay between the palpable and the abstract, the individual and the collective, echoing the philosophical inquiries of Marxist tenets of dialectical and historical materialism. Through a projects, I navigate of contemporary existence, wielding art as a catalyst for introspection, critique, and societal metamorphosis.

In visual endeavors I delve into the contradictions inherent in contemporary life, excavating the tensions between individual agency and societal constructs. The solemn portrayal of the regal figure in Moral Combat series serves as a poignant reflection on the human toll exacted by societal discord, while "Non-fungible You" offers a glimpse into the emancipatory potential of collective action and shared consciousness.

Through each project, I seek to unravel the intricacies of our era, forging connections between the personal and the universal, the mundane and the metaphysical, in pursuit of deeper understanding and cultural resonance.
